Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

FileMint is a local file utility for iPhone. The app does not require an account and does not operate a backend service.

Data Collection

FileMint does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. Files selected in the app are processed locally on your device.

Local Storage

Processed files and recent file history are stored in the app's local container on your device. You control any exported or shared files through iOS share options.

Network Access

The app does not actively send network requests for file processing, analytics, ads, or tracking. Opening the Privacy Policy, Support page, or email support is initiated by you.

Tracking and Analytics

FileMint does not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
